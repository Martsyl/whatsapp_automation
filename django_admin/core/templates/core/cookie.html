01 What Are Cookies 🍪

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide useful information to operators.

🔑 Login session identifiers
⚙️ User preferences
🛡️ Security tokens
📊 Website usage data
⏱️
Session Cookies
Erased when you close your browser. Used to maintain your login during a visit.
💾
Persistent Cookies
Remain on your device for a set period. Remember preferences across sessions.

04 Managing Cookies 🎛️

Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies. You can delete existing cookies, block new ones, or receive a notification when cookies are set.

🌐
Chrome
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
🦊
Firefox
Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
🧭
Safari
Preferences → Privacy → Manage Cookies
🔷
Edge
Settings → Privacy → Cookies & Site Data
🔴
Opera
Settings → Privacy → Cookies
📱
Mobile
Browser app → Settings → Privacy
⚠️ Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging into BotMart. Non-essential cookies can be disabled without affecting your account access.
