01 Overview 🏢

BotMart ("we", "our", or "us") is a WhatsApp automation platform operated in Nigeria. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our clients and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By registering for and using BotMart, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 Information We Collect 📋
  • Account Information: Business name, email address, WhatsApp number, and encrypted password collected at registration.
  • Meta / WhatsApp Credentials: Your Meta access token, Phone Number ID, and WABA ID — stored securely and used solely to operate your bot.
  • Business Content: Auto-reply rules, product listings, message templates, business hours, and AI business descriptions.
  • Contact Data: Phone numbers and names of your WhatsApp contacts who interact with your bot.
  • Message Logs: Inbound and outbound WhatsApp messages processed through your bot for analytics and debugging.
  • Payment Information: Transaction references, subscription plan, and payment amounts via Paystack. Card details are handled entirely by Paystack — we never see them.
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, and activity logs for security and performance monitoring.
03 How We Use Your Data ⚙️
  • To operate and deliver the BotMart WhatsApp automation service
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (reminders, receipts, account alerts)
  • To power AI Smart Reply using your business description and product data
  • To provide customer support and respond to enquiries
  • To improve our platform through aggregated, anonymised analytics
  • To comply with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations
✅ We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

05 WhatsApp & Meta Data 📱

BotMart integrates with the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta. By connecting your WhatsApp Business Account, you acknowledge that message data flows through Meta's infrastructure and is governed by Meta's Data Policy in addition to this policy.

Your access token grants BotMart permission to send and receive WhatsApp messages on your behalf. You can revoke this at any time by updating your Meta credentials or deactivating your account.

09 Cookies 🍪

BotMart uses session cookies to keep you logged in during your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this will prevent you from logging in.

10 Children's Privacy 👶

BotMart is a business tool intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us and we will promptly delete their account.
